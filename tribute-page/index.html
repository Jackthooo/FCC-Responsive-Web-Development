Alex G's timeline

  • 1983: born in Havertown, Pennsylvania.
  • 2004: learned to play his brother's guitar and started recording music at age 11
  • 2007-2011: in high school, he created two albums. One with the band MOTHER and another with his band the Skin Cells
  • 2011: enrolled at Temple University studying English, with the hopes of becoming a teacher. He dropped out to pursue his music career.
  • 2014: signed with Orchid Tapes, and released his label debut, DSU, along with reissues of his albums Rules and Trick
  • 2015: signed with Domino record label, and released the album Beach Music
  • 2017: releases Rocket, and garners critical acclaim
  • 2019: releases House of Sugar
  • 2022: release of the film "We're All Going to the World's Fair", which Alex scored
  • 2022: releases God Save the Animals
